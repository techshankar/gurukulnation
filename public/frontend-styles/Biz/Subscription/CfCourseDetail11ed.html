Course Description

Welcome to our specially crafted WordPress course, tailored for individuals seeking to master website development from the ground up while establishing their own lucrative home-based web designing business. We understand that starting a business can be overwhelming, especially for beginners yearning to earn money but unsure of where and how to embark on their entrepreneurial journey. Look no further, as web design ventures are incredibly profitable and offer unparalleled career opportunities. The course is specifically designed to cater to those without any programming knowledge. Yes, you read that right! With WordPress, even novices can effortlessly create stunning websites. What's more, the entire course is delivered in Hindi, ensuring easy comprehension for Hindi-speaking individuals who find it more convenient to learn in their native language. Who is this course for, you ask? Well, if you fall into any of the following categories, then this is the perfect course for you: Individuals aspiring to become web designers without any coding background. Those keen on mastering eCommerce development without the need for coding expertise. People seeking to dedicate just 1-2 hours per day to generate a valuable side income. Students looking to learn web design during their spare time, enhancing their skillset. Workers in search of an additional stream of income by venturing into the exciting world of web designing and development.
