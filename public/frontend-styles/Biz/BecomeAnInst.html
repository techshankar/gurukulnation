The exposure that I got due to Bizgurukul’s audience has helped me reach out to thousands of students all across India, gave me recognition and exposure, and the determination to consistently deliver the best for students and help them learn different skills. Really grateful for the opportunity by Bizgurukul and for showing trust in my abilities when I was at one of the lowest points in my life business-wise.
