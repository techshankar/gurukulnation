Course Description

As we all know English language is a very important part of communication not only in professional but in the personal life also, English has been an obstacle in the career growth of many job holders, businessmen, students etc.  Housewives also try to avoid visiting parent-teacher meetings as they feel uncomfortable when teachers at the meeting talk in English. The people who want to settle abroad need to clear IELTS & the students who are studying hard to obtain good scores in competitive exams, in their life English remains a barrier.

But now, this problem is coming to an end as this English Language course will enable the learners to write, to understand & to speak English with confidence and without hesitation. Gaurav has experience of more than 22 years of teaching English & has designed this course using his creativity of teaching with fun and has kept it very simple & easy to understand with step-by-step techniques keeping all the things in mind what exactly a learner needs from basic to advanced level.
