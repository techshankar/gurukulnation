Course Description

Microsoft Office Excel is the most popular spreadsheet tools available in the market. Excel Professionals are in high demand and learning excel is one of the best decisions you will make as it is a highly paid skill. It is a must know tool if you want to work in a corporate, or you are looking to develop a freelancer profile and earn sitting at home. It opens up many freelancing opportunities in many sectors such as finance, accounting, marketing, project management, data analysis and many more fields. No matter which industry it is excel is a must. It's employed for budgeting, financial management, project planning, inventory management, supply chain management and reporting etc. With charting and statistical functions, Excel aids data exploration and offers basic database capabilities. Even if you are not a professional, Microsoft Office Excel can help you in data organization, data manipulation, data cleaning, calculations, analysis, and visualization.

This course is organized in such an easy to learn way, that even if you are a complete beginner who just knows how to switch on a computer and use a mouse. You will be able to learn all the essential skills and apply for a job or as a freelancer and earn your way in this digital age.

The course is focused on fundamentals, as well developing a real-life skillset, using the practical hands on. I have provided some course material in pdf form.
