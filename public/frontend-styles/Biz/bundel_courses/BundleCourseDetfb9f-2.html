Course Description

Microsoft Office PowerPoint is the most popular presentation tools available in the market. It is a must know tool if you want to work in a corporate, or you are looking to develop a freelancer profile and earn sitting at home. It opens up many freelancing opportunities. You can do a lot of graphical, animated explainer video or designing work in PowerPoint. Even if you are not a professional, Microsoft Office PowerPoint can help you create interactive communicative presentation for any type of audience and can help you improve your confidence, organizing and presentation skills.

This course is organized in such an easy to learn way, that even if you are a complete beginner who just knows how to switch on a computer and use a mouse. You will be able to learn all the essential skills and apply for a job or as a freelancer and earn your way in this digital age.

The course is focused on fundamentals, as well developing a real-life skillset, using the practical hands on. I have provided some course material in pdf form. Also, I have provided in the course all you need to know for becoming a freelancer, where you can get the free and paid templates and where you can avail all these opportunities as a pdf file to help you start your own freelancing business.
